Why this project?

The Hebrew Heritage Project began with a father's desire to teach the Bible faithfully to his children. What started as a simple question while reading a picture Bible became a journey through language, culture and Scripture. The project explores how Ancient Hebrew may be preserved within the languages of Africa and invites readers to examine the linguistic evidence for themselves.
