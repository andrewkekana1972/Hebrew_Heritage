The Hebrew Heritage of Bantu Languages

Introduction

This website documents an ongoing investigation into the preservation of Ancient Hebrew vocabulary among the Bantu languages of Africa. The accompanying Hebrew–Bantu Dictionary allows readers to explore lexical relationships interactively.
